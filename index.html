About Me

Hey everyone! Are you ready to capture the beautiful moments in your life?

Let me introduce myself!
My name is Sarah, and I’m the creative soul behind Radiant Aura Photography.
My photography journey began back in year 10, but it wasn’t until I got my first camera at 18 that my passion truly blossomed.

Living in stunning Far North Queensland opened my eyes to nature's breathtaking beauty, which inspired me to pursue a diploma in photography.
Since then, I've been capturing life one click at a time!

Recently, I decided to shift my focus from landscapes to families. Whether it's maternity shoots, newborns, family portraits, engagements, or even boudoir—I'm here for it all! Your family's spirit deserves to be captured beautifully.

For peace of mind when working with children, I'm fully vaccinated (including whooping cough) and hold a current blue card as I'm studying primary education.
Safety is always a priority! I can’t wait to help you create lasting memories through my lens!

If you're interested in booking a session or just want to say hi, message me!
